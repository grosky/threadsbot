Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 May 2026

1. Who we are

Lazy Threads (the «Service») is an AI assistant for Threads content creators, delivered as a Telegram bot under the handle @aithreadbot. The Service is operated by an independent developer and is not affiliated with Meta Platforms, Inc.

2. What data we collect

When you use the Service, we collect and store the following data:

  • Telegram account data: your Telegram user ID, username, first name — required to identify you across sessions.
  • Onboarding answers: information you voluntarily provide about your niche, target audience, product, tone of voice, etc. Used solely to personalise generated posts.
  • Threads account data: when you connect Threads via OAuth, we store your Threads user ID, username and access token. The access token is encrypted at rest using symmetric encryption (Fernet).
  • Generated content: posts created by the AI on your behalf, cached for up to 24 hours so you can publish them later.
  • Usage logs: timestamps of generations, publications and other actions, used for daily limits and analytics.

3. How we use your data

  • To generate posts tailored to your niche and audience
  • To publish posts to Threads when you explicitly tap «Publish»
  • To enforce subscription limits and prevent abuse
  • To send you transactional notifications (e.g. subscription status, referral rewards)

We never sell your data, never share it with advertisers, and never use it to train AI models.

4. Third-party services

The Service interacts with the following third parties on your behalf:

  • Google Gemini API — for AI text generation. Your prompts (onboarding info + post topic) are sent to Google. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Meta Threads API — for OAuth authentication and content publishing. See Meta Privacy Policy.
  • Telegram Bot API — as the messaging delivery channel. See Telegram Privacy Policy.
  • Railway — for application hosting and database storage.

5. Data storage and security

  • Data is stored in a SQLite database on a Railway server
  • OAuth access tokens are encrypted at rest
  • Access to the database is restricted to the application server
  • HTTPS is used for all OAuth callbacks

6. Data retention

  • Account data persists until you disconnect Threads or stop using the Service
  • Generated post cache auto-deletes after 24 hours
  • You may request full deletion of all your data at any time (see Contact below)

7. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Revoke Threads access via the /menu command in the bot or via Threads settings
  • Stop using the Service at any time

8. Data Deletion

You can request full deletion of all data associated with your account at any time. There are two ways:

  • Inside the bot: open @aithreadbot and send the command /delete_me. We will permanently delete your profile, onboarding answers, generated content, Threads access tokens and all usage logs within 30 days.
  • By email: write to tomyhellatriger@gmail.com from any address, including in the body your Telegram username or Telegram user ID. We will confirm deletion within 30 days.

Deletion is irreversible. Threads access tokens are revoked from our side; if you want to revoke the permission grant on Meta's side as well, visit threads.com/settings/account/apps.

9. Children

The Service is not intended for users under 18 years of age.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated via the bot.

11. Contact

For privacy questions, data requests or deletion: contact the developer via Telegram: @grosky or by email at tomyhellatriger@gmail.com.
